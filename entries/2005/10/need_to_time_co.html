Need to time contractions?

Today Mariah had some practice contractions, and writing down approximate starting times and durations was just too much work and not as precise as I wanted it to be. So this afternoon I built a little tool to help me. Now before you think I'm a totally nerdy looser, know that it only took me about 20 minutes, building Flash applications is what I do for a living. Anyways...introducing...

The Contraction Counter

Instructions: click start when a contraction starts, stop when it ends. Repeat. When you think it's time to call your doctor or go to the hospital or whatever, print it out and take it with you. That's it!

There might be some little bugs. If you want the source files for some reason, put a comment below and I'll mail them to you.
