Hey, why didn't they interview me?

The New York Times article Web Sites for the Brave New Electronic B@by seems written just for us (Guido Jack had his domain well before he was born).

Hopefully he'll thank me for all this in a few years (instead of realizing what a nerd his dad is...). ;)
